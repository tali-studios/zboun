### 1. BACKGROUND

1.1 Operator provides a software platform and related services that enable restaurants to publish digital menus, receive customer communications (including via third-party messaging services), and use related tools made available by Operator from time to time (**“Platform”**).  

1.2 Restaurant wishes to access the Platform and related services in connection with its food and beverage (or related) business, subject to this Agreement.

---

### 2. DEFINITIONS

**2.1** **“Account”** means the credentials and access profile Operator issues to Restaurant to use the Platform.  

**2.2** **“Customer Data”** means information relating to end customers that Restaurant or its personnel submit, upload, or generate through the Platform.  

**2.3** **“Restaurant Content”** means menus, images, descriptions, prices, trademarks, logos, and any other materials Restaurant provides or makes available through the Platform.  

**2.4** **“Services”** means the Platform, Account, support (if any), and ancillary services described on Operator’s then-current website, order form, or written addendum signed by the Parties.  

**2.5** **“Subscription Term”** means the initial subscription period and any renewal period agreed in writing or through the Platform checkout flow.

---

### 3. GRANT OF ACCESS; ACCOUNT PROVISION

**3.1 Grant.** Subject to this Agreement and timely payment of applicable fees, Operator grants Restaurant a **non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable** right to access and use the Services during the Subscription Term solely for Restaurant’s internal business purposes.  

**3.2 Account issuance.** Operator will provide an Account **only after** (a) execution of this Agreement (or click-through acceptance, if you use that method and counsel approves), and (b) completion of any onboarding steps Operator reasonably requires (including identity or business verification «OPTIONAL»).  

**3.3 Administrators.** Restaurant shall designate authorized users (**“Administrators”**) and shall ensure Administrators comply with this Agreement. Restaurant is **fully responsible** for all activity under its Account, whether or not authorized.

---

### 4. RESTAURANT OBLIGATIONS

**4.1 Accurate information.** Restaurant shall provide accurate, complete, and current registration, tax, contact, and payment information and shall update it promptly.  

**4.2 Lawful use.** Restaurant shall use the Services only in compliance with applicable law, including consumer protection, advertising, pricing, food safety (where applicable), data protection, and **anti-spam / electronic communications** rules.  

**4.3 Messaging and orders.** Where the Services integrate with third-party messaging (e.g. WhatsApp) or similar channels:  
(a) Restaurant acknowledges it is responsible for the **lawfulness and content** of messages sent to its customers;  
(b) Restaurant shall obtain and maintain any **consents** required to contact individuals;  
(c) Restaurant shall not use the Services to send unlawful, deceptive, harassing, or bulk unsolicited communications.  

**4.4 Restaurant Content.** Restaurant represents and warrants that it owns or has obtained all rights necessary for Restaurant Content and that Restaurant Content does not infringe third-party rights or violate law.  

**4.5 Security.** Restaurant shall safeguard Account credentials, use strong passwords, and notify Operator promptly of any suspected compromise.

### 5. OPERATOR OBLIGATIONS

**5.1 Services.** Operator will use commercially reasonable efforts to make the Services available in accordance with its published or agreed service descriptions.  

**5.2 Support.** Unless otherwise agreed, support shall be provided on a **reasonable-efforts** basis via channels Operator designates.  

**5.3 Changes.** Operator may modify features that do not materially reduce core functionality, or may modify the Platform for security, legal, or technical reasons, with **reasonable notice** where practicable.

**6.4 No deduction.** Restaurant shall pay all amounts **without set-off** or withholding unless required by law.
